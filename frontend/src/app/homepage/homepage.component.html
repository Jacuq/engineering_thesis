Music theory for guitar - training app

About app

This app was made with an intention to help all aspiring guitar players with visualising and practising music theory. To navigate use the navbar on the top of the page. The site features:

  • Virtual acoustic guitar
  • Virtual bass guitar
  • Ear training tool
  • Quiz testing knowledge of scales and chords
App was entirely developed by Jacek Kozicki as an engineering thesis project.
